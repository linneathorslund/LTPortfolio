Biografen i Bergsjön

  • UI/UX
  • Film

  • Graphic design
Poster made for a market research in Bergsjön, Gothenburg. The research was made on behalf of the non-profit association Navet in Bergsjön.
As a student in Kulturverkstan, I was a part of the student group that was assigned this project. The poster was made as marketing material for the research. It was put up around Bergsjön, as well as online. The illustrative style was later reused in the material that presented the conclusion of the project.

The poster was made with Adobe Illustrator as primary tool to create the illustrations and Adoe Photoshop was used to composite the whole illustration.

An alternative design was made to use as front page for the project report.
